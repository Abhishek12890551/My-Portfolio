WELCOME

Hello! I'm Abhishek Kumar, a B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering student at Mahatma Gandhi Central University. I'm a passionate game development enthusiast and aspiring game designer on a journey to craft immersive digital experiences. I'm currently honing my skills in C, C#, HTML & CSS,Unity, Unreal Engine, and Blender.

When I'm not immersed in the world of game development, you can find me lost in the pages of captivating sci-fi novels or engrossed in cinematic adventures. Music is my constant companion, providing the soundtrack to my creative process. Beyond the digital realm, I'm a fervent football player, finding joy in the exhilarating rush of the game. Join me as I combine my love for technology, storytelling, and sports to create exciting worlds and unforgettable gaming experiences.
